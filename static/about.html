Meet the Experts

The brilliant minds behind W3MG who make the magic happen every single day.

Director

Mayuresh G

Director

Mayuresh Goyal is an accomplished Tech Lead, Director, and Full-Stack Senior Engineer with over 15 years of experience building and scaling complex digital platforms across healthcare, pharmaceutical technology, eCommerce, and enterprise CRM systems. He brings a strong track record of delivering secure, highly scalable, and compliance-ready solutions, combining deep hands-on engineering expertise with strategic technical leadership in both regulated and high-growth environments.

Mayuresh has architected and led the development of high-traffic, highly scalable WooCommerce platforms and custom digital commerce ecosystems built on WordPress, Laravel-based backend services, and MERN-driven frontend architectures. His work emphasizes performance optimization, horizontal scalability, fault tolerance, and system decoupling, enabling platforms to support large product catalogs, high concurrency, and multi-region deployments. Leveraging AWS for cloud infrastructure and Vercel for modern frontend delivery, he has designed deployment pipelines and runtime environments optimized for speed, reliability, and continuous delivery at scale.

In the healthcare and medical technology domains, Mayuresh has served as a compliance-focused engineering leader, overseeing the technical implementation of HIPAA and PIPEDA compliance through Vanta-based automation and monitoring. He has embedded security-by-design and privacy-by-default principles into application architecture and engineering workflows, overseeing data protection controls, identity and access management, audit logging, incident response processes, and continuous compliance validation. His cross-functional leadership with legal, security, and operations teams has enabled sustained audit readiness while maintaining rapid development velocity.

With a proven ability to execute across the full product lifecycle, Mayuresh excels in end-to-end platform ownership, from early-stage architecture and system design through development, deployment, compliance certification, and long-term optimization. His leadership has directly contributed to multiple startups achieving multi-million-dollar revenue growth, particularly in the healthcare and e-commerce sectors. Driven by a passion for solving real-world problems through technology, Mayuresh consistently aligns technical strategy, regulatory requirements, and business objectives, making him a valuable asset to organizations operating at scale and under regulatory scrutiny.

Our Process

A mobile-native design process built for speed, iteration, and platform excellence.

1

Research & Architecture

User research, competitive analysis, and information architecture tailored for mobile contexts — small screens, one-handed use, and on-the-go interactions.

2

Wireframe & Flow

Low-fidelity screen flows mapping every user journey, edge case, and empty state — validated with stakeholders before visual design begins.

3

UI Design & Motion

High-fidelity screens with micro-interactions, transition animations, and platform-specific patterns — prototyped for realistic device testing.

4

Test & Handoff

On-device usability testing, accessibility audits, and comprehensive developer handoff with component specs, assets, and interaction documentation.

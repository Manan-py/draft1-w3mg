HIPAA-Minded Web Engineering: A Practical Checklist

w3mg 📅 March 22, 2026 ⏱ 7 min read

This article shares engineering practices we use when building web applications that handle sensitive health information. It is not legal advice; always involve qualified counsel and compliance officers for your jurisdiction and use case.

Encryption and transport

TLS everywhere, modern cipher suites, and HSTS on public endpoints. Secrets live in a vault or managed secret store — never in Git or client-side bundles.

Access control and least privilege

Role-based access, short-lived tokens, and separate credentials per environment. Admin actions should be attributable to a user with multi-factor authentication where appropriate.

  • Audit logs for reads and writes on sensitive records.
  • Backups encrypted at rest with tested restore procedures.
  • Vendor due diligence documented (BAA where applicable).

Shipping culture

Security reviews for new features, dependency updates on a schedule, and incident runbooks reduce panic when something goes wrong. We treat compliance as ongoing operations, not a one-time checkbox.
